About CalcKaro

CalcKaro is a free financial calculator platform built specifically for Indian investors, taxpayers, salaried employees, and home buyers. Our mission is simple: give every Indian instant, accurate, transparent financial math — without paywalls, logins, or tracking.

Why we built this

Most financial calculators in India are buried inside brokerage websites, bank portals, or finance apps that exist to sell you something. The math is hidden, the inputs are vague, and the outputs are designed to nudge you toward a product — not to inform you.

We built CalcKaro to be different. Every calculator here shows you the actual formula. Every input is yours alone — we don't store your salary, your loan amount, or your tax data anywhere. Every result is computed instantly in your browser, with no API calls, no subscriptions, and no login.

What we believe

  • Financial math should be free — Compounding, EMI, taxation — these are basic arithmetic that every Indian deserves to see clearly.
  • Privacy by default — Your financial inputs are too sensitive to leave your browser. We never see them.
  • India first — Tax slabs, deductions, and investment products are Indian-specific. We don't ship generic global calculators.
  • Honest content — We refresh logic every budget season. When slabs change, our calculators change too.

Our team

CalcKaro is built and maintained by a small team of engineers and finance writers based in India. We have over a decade of experience building consumer fintech products and Indian tax tools. We are not Chartered Accountants — our content is for educational and informational purposes only. For binding tax advice, please consult a CA.

How CalcKaro stays free

CalcKaro is funded by non-intrusive display advertising (Google AdSense) and, occasionally, affiliate links to financial products we genuinely recommend. We never promote a product we don't believe in, and we never charge users for calculator access. Premium features — if we ever build them — will always have a free tier.
