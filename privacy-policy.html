Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Our commitment

CalcKaro is built on a privacy-first principle: your financial inputs never leave your browser. Every calculation runs as JavaScript on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any financial information you enter into our calculators.

Information we collect

  • Calculator inputs: None. We never see what you type into a calculator.
  • Personal information: We do not require accounts, logins, or any personal information to use CalcKaro calculators.
  • Contact form: If you email us or use the contact form, we receive the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message) for the sole purpose of responding.
  • Analytics: We use privacy-friendly, anonymized analytics to count page views and understand which calculators are popular. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Cookies and advertising

CalcKaro uses Google AdSense to display ads. AdSense may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can manage your ad personalization preferences at adssettings.google.com.

Third-party services

  • Google AdSense — for displaying ads
  • Google Analytics (anonymized) — for traffic measurement

These services have their own privacy policies governing data they collect.

Children's privacy

CalcKaro is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the "Last updated" date.
